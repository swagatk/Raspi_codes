
Keep checking for a button press in a thread. Once the button is pressed, 
the following activities should take place. If the button is pressed again, the robot should stop moving. 


Step 1: Lift the arm UP (see `servo_test.py`). Start the camera and take the picture of two tags infront
 of the robot and save them as Home tag location. Time: ~ 10 seconds

Step 2: Rotate by 180 degrees to face opposite to the wall containing home tags. Time: ~ 5 seconds

Step 3:  Now scan for orange balls by rotating along a single direction (left or right) 
until balls are found (see `ball_detection.py`). ~ 20 seconds

step 3: Once balls are detected, move towards the balls while avoiding obstacles. 
Move along the direction having largest density of balls (more number of bounding boxes). 
If the balls are lost due to obstacle avoidance manouevre, it should slowly rotate to
look for balls again and move towards it once detected. (See `go_to_ball.py`). Time: ~ 60 seconds (1 min)


Step 4. Once it reaches the ball, Perform 'ball capture manoeuvre' which involves 
lowering the arm and move forward slowly while opening and closing the gripper at regular intervals (~ 3 sec)
for about 3-4 times. See `keyboard_control.py`, key command 'B'. Time: ~ 40 seconds

Step 5. Close the gripper one last time and move the arm UP. (See `servo_test.py` file): Time: ~ 5 seconds

Step 6. Look for home tag and move towards the home tag location (see 'go_to_home.py'). 
The robot should be stop about 50 cm away from the home wall. Time: ~ 1 minute (60 seconds)

Step 7. Once home is reached, execute 'Ball drop manoeuvre' which involves going to
 'ARM Drop' pose from `servo_test.py` file. Time: ~ 20 seconds 

Step 8: It should come to its rest pose with arm going down and motors off.
